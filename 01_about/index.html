WHAT ABOUT ME?

Born and raised in Milan, I attended the Politecnico di Milano studying Industrial Design and Communication Design.

I spent a period of study and work in Australia, and after my graduation I spent some time working on wayfinding at Mijksenaar in Amsterdam.

I am currently located in New York City, attending the MFA Design+Technology at Parsons.
