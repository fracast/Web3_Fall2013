GNOCCHI

THE INGREDIENTS

CHOOSE AND COOK THE POTATOES

Use red potatoes (one kilo for 4 people) and boiled them with their skin

MASH AND SEASON THE POTATOES

Peel the potatoes. Mash the potatoes.
Add 2 yolks. Season with salt, pepper, nutmeg.

WORK THE DOUGH

Amalgamate all the ingredients with flour in a smooth dough.
Roll in cilinders and cut in small bits.
Roll those bits between the fork and your finger
to give them the texture

COOK THE GNOCCHI

Cook the gnocchi a few at the time.
They are ready when they start floating on the boiling water.

TOP IT WITH AWESOME SAUCE

Now drown them in your favorite sauce!
